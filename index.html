About Me

Github Resume Instagram

Hi my name is Brad Meyn and I'm an aspiring web developer living in Newcastle, Australia. I have a background in finance with a double degree from the Univerity of Newcastle.

Initally I became interested in web development purely as a hobby. For my work I'd create different spreadsheets for investment comparisons or modelling different scenarios. After making these tools I began to wonder if I could replicate some of them on a website. As a curiosity I completed an online web development bootcamp and started to immerse myself in the world of web development.

Going from knowing nothing about programming to being able to build things on the web has been a very rewarding process. After squeezing in development time around work and family I've come to the realisation this is what I want to do for a career. The purpose of me making this portfolio site is to highlight what I know and obtain a development job.
